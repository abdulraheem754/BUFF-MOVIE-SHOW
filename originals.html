Web Series:"The Originals"


Director:Julie Plec,L.J. Smith,Michael Karasick,Chris Grismer
Writers:Julie Plec
Stars: Joseph Morgan, Daniel Gillies, Claire Holt,Phoebe Tonkin,Charles Michael, Leah Pipes.
IMDb RATING: ⭐ 8.43/10
Runtime: 42 to 45 minutes Per Episode

Description :"The Originals" is a supernatural drama series focusing on the Mikaelson siblings—Klaus, Elijah, and Rebekah, who are the original vampires. The show is set in New Orleans and follows their return to reclaim control of the city, which they helped build centuries ago. As they navigate complex relationships and power struggles with other supernatural beings—vampires, witches, and werewolves—they face internal and external conflicts. The series explores themes of family loyalty, power, redemption, and the consequences of immortality, blending drama and supernatural intrigue throughout its five-season run.

Klaus Mikaelson
Actor:Joseph Morgan

Background:Niklaus Mikaelson, or Klaus, is the hybrid of a vampire and a werewolf, making him one of the most powerful and feared beings in the supernatural world. Born in the 10th century, Klaus is known for his volatile temperament, ambition, and complex family dynamics.

Role in the Story :Klaus seeks to reclaim control of New Orleans, a city he helped build, and protect his family. His character is marked by his internal struggle between his ruthless desire for power and his deep, though often troubled, love for his family, especially his daughter with Hayley.

Elijah Mikaelson
Actor:Daniel Gillies

Background: Elijah Mikaelson is the noble and honorable older brother of Klaus. Known for his strong moral compass and sense of duty, Elijah is committed to maintaining order and protecting his family.

Role in the Story: As a key leader within the Mikaelson family, Elijah often acts as a mediator and a stabilizing force. His character is driven by his quest for redemption and his efforts to balance his family's needs with the challenges posed by the supernatural community.

Rebekah Mikaelson
Actor:Claire Holt

Background: Rebekah Mikaelson is the only sister of Klaus and Elijah. She has a deep-seated desire for family unity and security, often feeling overshadowed by her brothers' dominant personalities.

Role in the Story:Rebekah’s storyline focuses on her quest for a stable and loving family life. Her vulnerability and longing for acceptance play a significant role in her decisions and relationships throughout the series.

Hayley Marshall
Actor:Phoebe Tonkin

Background: Hayley Marshall is a werewolf who becomes romantically involved with Klaus and later becomes the mother of his daughter, Hope. She is a fierce and independent character with a strong sense of loyalty.

Role in the Story: Hayley’s role involves navigating her complex relationship with Klaus, protecting her child, and dealing with the supernatural politics of New Orleans. She evolves from a lone wolf to a key player in the Mikaelson family’s struggles.

Camille O'Connell
Actor:Leah Pipes

Background:Camille O'Connell is a psychology student who becomes entangled with the Mikaelson family. She is intelligent, compassionate, and often serves as a moral compass for those around her.

Role in the Story: Camille's involvement with the supernatural world and her relationship with Klaus make her a central character in exploring the more human and emotional aspects of the supernatural conflicts.

Marcel Gerard
Actor:Charles Michael Davis

Background: Marcel Gerard is a vampire who was once Klaus’s protégé but has since become the dominant vampire leader in New Orleans. He is known for his charisma and leadership skills.

Role in the Story:Marcel's leadership and control of the vampire community create tension with Klaus, leading to power struggles and conflicts. His character is a major player in the city's supernatural politics.
