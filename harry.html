Movie Name:"HARRY POTTER"


Director: Chris Columbus
Writers: J.K. RowlingSteve Kloves
Stars: Daniel Radcliffe,Rupert Grin,tEmma Watson
IMDb RATING: ⭐ 7.6/10
Runtime: 2 hours 32 minutes

Description : Harry Potter is the protagonist of J.K. Rowling's "Harry Potter" series. He is an orphaned wizard who discovers his magical heritage on his 11th birthday and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Known for his distinctive lightning-shaped scar and bravery, Harry learns about his past, uncovers his connection to the dark wizard Voldemort, and becomes central to the battle against evil forces threatening the wizarding world. His journey is marked by friendship, courage, and the struggle to protect those he loves.

HARRY POTTER
Actor: Daniel Radcliffe

Full Name: Harry James Potter

Birthdate: July 31, 1980

House: Gryffindor

Background: Harry is the orphaned son of James and Lily Potter, who were murdered by Lord Voldemort when Harry was just a baby. Harry survived the attack due to his mother's sacrifice, leaving him with a lightning-shaped scar on his forehead. Raised by his unkind relatives, the Dursleys, Harry learns he is a wizard on his eleventh birthday. At Hogwarts, he becomes known for his bravery, resilience, and innate talent for magic. Over the course of the series, Harry battles against Voldemort and his followers, uncovering truths about his past and his destiny.

Role in the Story : As the protagonist of the series, Harry's journey spans seven years at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he learns about his magical abilities, uncovers hidden truths about his parents, and battles the forces of darkness led by Voldemort.

Hermione Granger
Actor: Emma Watson

Full Name: Hermione Jean Granger

Birthday: September 19, 1979

House: Gryffindor

Background: Muggle-born witch with non-magical parents. Known for her exceptional intelligence and academic prowess. She discovers her magical abilities at a young age and excels at Hogwarts.

Role in the Story: Harry’s best friend and one of his most reliable allies. Hermione’s intellect and knowledge are crucial in solving problems, uncovering secrets, and assisting in the fight against Voldemort. She is also a key member of Dumbledore’s Army and later becomes an advocate for magical creature rights.

Ron Weasley
Actor:Rupert Grint

Full Name: Ronald Bilius Weasley

Birthday: March 1, 1980

House: Gryffindor

Background: The sixth of seven children in the Weasley family, known for their warmth and modest means. Ron comes from a loving but financially struggling family. He is often overshadowed by his older siblings but possesses a strong sense of loyalty and humor.

Role in the Story: Harry’s other best friend and a crucial part of the trio along with Hermione. Ron’s bravery, loyalty, and practical skills are vital in their adventures and fight against Voldemort. He provides emotional support and comic relief, and his family’s connections are instrumental in the overall battle.

Albus Dumbledore
Actor:Richard Harris, Michael Gambon, and Jude Law

Full Name: Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore

Birthday: c. 1881

House: Gryffindor

Background: One of the most powerful and respected wizards, known for his role as Headmaster of Hogwarts. He has a complex history, including his past friendship with the dark wizard Gellert Grindelwald and his troubled family background.

Role in the Story: Mentor to Harry Potter, guiding him through his journey and providing crucial information about Voldemort and the Horcruxes. His strategic planning and sacrifices are key to the eventual defeat of Voldemort.

Tom Riddle / Lord Voldemort
Actor:Christian Peter Coulson/Ralph Fiennes

Full Name: Tom Marvolo Riddle (later known as Lord Voldemort)

Birthday: December 31, 1926

House: Slytherin

Background: Born as Tom Marvolo Riddle, the son of a Muggle father and a witch mother. His early life was marked by hardship, and his obsession with conquering death and achieving immortality led him to dark magic and the creation of Horcruxes.

Role in the Story: The primary antagonist of the series. Voldemort seeks to dominate the wizarding world and eliminate those who oppose him. His actions drive the central conflict of the series, culminating in his final confrontation with Harry Potter.

Rubeus Hagrid
Actor:Robbie Coltrane

Full Name: Rubeus Hagrid

Birthday: December 6, 1928

House:

Background: A half-giant who works as Keeper of Keys and Grounds at Hogwarts. He was expelled from Hogwarts in his third year but later becomes a beloved figure at the school. Known for his love of magical creatures and his large size.

Role in the Story: Hagrid is Harry’s first magical friend and mentor. He introduces Harry to the wizarding world and provides emotional and practical support throughout the series. His loyalty and kindness make him a central figure in Harry’s life.

Severus Snape
Actor:Alan Rickman

Full Name: Severus Snape

Birthday: January 9, 1960

House: Slytherin

Background: Son of a Muggle father and a witch mother, Snape had a difficult and troubled childhood. He was a former Death Eater who later became a double agent, working for both Voldemort and Dumbledore. His complicated past is revealed throughout the series.

Role in the Story: Initially portrayed as an antagonist, Snape’s true allegiance and sacrifices are revealed posthumously. He plays a crucial role in protecting Harry and ensuring Voldemort’s defeat, driven by his love for Lily Potter.

These character bios provide a comprehensive look at the main figures in the Harry Potter series, highlighting their backgrounds and roles within the story.
