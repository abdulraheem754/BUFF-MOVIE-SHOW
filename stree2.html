"Stree 2:Sarkate Ka Aatank"


Director:Amar Kaushik
Writers: Niren Bhatt
Stars: Rajkummar Rao, Shraddha Kapoor,Pankaj Trapathi,Abhishek Banarjee,Aparshakti Khurana.
IMDb RATING: ⭐ 7.6/10
Runtime: 2 hours 27 minutes

Description : "Stree 2" continues the story of a small town haunted by a female spirit targeting men. The original characters, including Vicky and his friends, reunite to uncover the truth behind the legend and protect their community. The film blends humor and horror, featuring new characters and challenges while exploring themes of bravery and friendship. It's a fun mix of laughs and thrills, building on the charm of the first movie.

Vicky Raghuwanshi
Actor:Rajkummar Rao

Background:Vicky is a young, resourceful man who gained notoriety in his town after facing the mysterious spirit in the first film. His experiences have made him both brave and slightly paranoid, as he grapples with the lingering fear of the supernatural.

Role in the Story : As the protagonist, Vicky is the central figure in rallying his friends and the community against the spirit’s return. His wit and humor help lighten tense situations, but he also shows growth as he learns to confront his fears more directly. His determination to protect his loved ones drives much of the plot.

Bittu Shukla
Actor:Aparshakti Khurana

Background:Vicky’s childhood friend, Bittu is characterized by his comedic outlook and somewhat skeptical nature regarding the supernatural. He often provides comic relief through his exaggerated reactions and witty one-liners.

Role in the Story: Bittu’s loyalty to Vicky is unwavering, and although he often doubts their chances against the spirit, he ultimately joins the fight. His character illustrates the importance of friendship and support in overcoming fears, and he evolves from comic relief to a more integral part of the team.

Jana Sinha
Actor:Abhishek Banarjee

Background:Jana is a local resident of the small town, known for his lighthearted nature and quirky personality. He embodies the typical small-town character, deeply influenced by local superstitions and folklore.

Role in the Story:Jana serves as a friend to Vicky and Bittu, often adding humor to tense situations. His exaggerated beliefs about the spirit create comedic moments while also highlighting the town's superstitions. Throughout the film, Jana participates in the group's efforts to confront the haunting, showcasing loyalty and a willingness to face danger alongside his friends.

Rudra Pratap Singh
Actor:Pankaj Tripathi

Background: Rudra is a local perosn with deep roots in the town’s culture and folklore. He is known for his superstitions and belief in the supernatural, having witnessed the impact of the spirit on the community.

Role in the Story: As a central character, Rudra provides comic relief and sage advice to the younger protagonists, Vicky and his friends. His understanding of the town’s history and legends is crucial in navigating the challenges posed by the spirit. Rudra often serves as a bridge between the humorous and serious aspects of the story.

Unnamed Woman
Actor:Shraddha Kapoor

Role in the Story: Shraddha Kapoor plays a crucial role in the Stree Universe. In the first film, she helped Vicky and his team defeat Stree, later vanishing with Stree’s braided hair. She returns in Stree 2 to help the team defeat Sar Kata and reveals herself as Stree’s daughter.

Sar Kata
Actor:Sunil Kumar

Role in the Story: Sarkata is a supernatural villain who causes havoc in the town of Chanderi. He is the vengeful spirit of Chandrabhan, a former village head who murdered Stree, a courtesan. The ghost-fighting hero Stree returns to face off against Sarkata in this sequel

These characters collectively drive the narrative, blending humor, horror, and themes of friendship and bravery throughout "Stree 2."
