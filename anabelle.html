Movie Name:"ANABELLE"


Director: Dani Geddes
Writers: Gary Dauberman
Stars: Amara Clarke,Tilliah Gard, Lamissah La-Shontae, Romy Stallard,Emily Twinn-Dorey,Angel-May Webb.
IMDb RATING: ⭐ 9.1/10
Runtime: 1 hours 39 minutes

Description :"Annabelle" is a 2014 horror film about a possessed doll that terrorizes a young couple. Set in the 1960s, the story follows Mia and John Form, who are haunted by supernatural events linked to the doll they received as a gift. The film explores themes of demonic possession and the battle between good and evil.

MIA FORM
Actor: Annabelle Wallis

Background: Mia is a pregnant woman who is excited about starting a family with her husband. She is a nurturing and caring individual.

Role in the Story : Mia is central to the film’s plot. After receiving the Annabelle doll as a gift, she begins experiencing disturbing and supernatural events. Her struggle to protect her unborn child and herself from the doll’s malevolent influence drives the narrative.

JOHN FORM
Actor: Ward Horton

Background: John is Mia's husband and works as a doctor. He is supportive and loving but initially skeptical of the supernatural occurrences that Mia reports.

Role in the Story: John’s role evolves from disbelief to active involvement in the fight against the evil forces connected to the Annabelle doll. His actions are crucial in trying to uncover the doll’s origins and protect his family.

Father Perez
Actor:Tony Amendola

Background: Father Perez is a priest with experience in dealing with supernatural phenomena. He is knowledgeable about demonic forces and exorcisms.

Role in the Story: Father Perez provides critical support to Mia and John. He helps them understand the nature of the evil tied to the Annabelle doll and offers spiritual guidance. His expertise is key in addressing the haunting.

Evelyn
Actor:Alfre Woodard

Background: Evelyn is a bookstore owner who encounters Mia and offers her support and friendship. She is compassionate and has a personal connection to the occult, which adds depth to her character.

Role in the Story: Evelyn helps Mia by providing emotional support and practical advice. Her background in the occult allows her to offer insights that are crucial for understanding the doll’s malevolent nature.

Annabelle (The Doll)
Actor:Tree O'Toole

Background: Annabelle is a vintage porcelain doll that is the focal point of the film’s horror. It originally belonged to a deceased member of a satanic cult.

Role in the Story: Annabelle serves as the conduit for demonic forces. Its unsettling presence and the supernatural occurrences tied to it drive the film’s horror elements. The doll is central to the plot as Mia and John struggle to rid their home of its dark influence.

Each character plays a distinct role in advancing the story and confronting the terror associated with the Annabelle doll.
